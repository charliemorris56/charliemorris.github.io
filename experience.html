Experance

Worked at IT Protocol for two years, then two years studying Computing Mathematics at the University of South Wales before that

IT Protocol 2018 - 2019

For the first year at IT Protocol I was an apprentice, and thus spent three weeks out of the year on a training course, I did my apprenticeship through 3AAA who went under mid was through my apprenticeship.

At the end of my apprenticeship, I told my boss about my aspirations to go to university and study games programming, my boss was happy to keep me on until then.

University of South Wales, Computing Mathematics 2014 - 2017

Before working at IT Protocol, I was at university studying Computing Mathematics, which was a joint course between computer science and mathematics, it was about 70% mathematics and and ran by the mathematics department.

Mathematics was my best subject at school and thus I choose to do it at A levels, which lead to me choosing to do it at university. I started to realise that I did not like any of the job prospects at the end of the course and I was also falling behind on some modules, this led me to drop out and pursue working with computers, at first I wanted to go into a programming apprenticeship but due to me having done some at university the government finance was not available.

Whilst at university I did a course creating a simple game on the Raspberry PI, it was the most fun and engaging course I did, this is what lead me to go back to university and try my hand at games programming.
